STEP 1: Tempel link target di kolom "Link Phishing Checker" pada beranda
STEP 2: Klik tombol "Analyze Link" warna biru

🟡 Waspada = Ada kata "login/verifikasi/dana kaget"
🔴 Bahaya = Domain aneh / tanpa HTTPS
